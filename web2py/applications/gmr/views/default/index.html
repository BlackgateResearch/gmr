Game Master Radio

GMR is a radio service designed especially for Game-Masters of table-top games. We provide a library of excellent, non-vocal music which you can tailor to suit your own game-world.

Music Library

GMR is building a world-class collection of non-vocal music especially aimed at table-top gaming. Our ever-expanding catalogue will allow you to choose the best music to compliment your games.

PASS Mood Selection

To make the process of finding the perfect music for your game as simple as possible, we have created the PASS system: each track has a user-voted score for Positivity, Aggression, Speed and Suspence making it possible to find tracks fitting over 5000 specific moods.

Composers!

Can you compose great, non-vocal music that table-top gamers would like to hear in their games? Do you want the chance to make money from your compositions? If so then GMR needs you!
